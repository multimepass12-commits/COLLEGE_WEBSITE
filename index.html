Diploma We Offer

Explore a diverse range of programs designed to foster academic excellence and professional growth.

Diploma in CMS & ED

This program equips students with essential medical knowledge and skills for community health services, focusing on primary care and essential drug management.

Diploma in Medical Lab Technician

Gain hands-on experience in our state-of-the-art labs. This diploma prepares you for a career in medical diagnostics, performing tests and analyzing samples.

Diploma in Ophthalmic Assistant

Specialize in eye care and assist ophthalmologists. This program covers vision testing, patient care, and the use of ophthalmic equipment.
