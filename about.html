About Us

We are the world's largest university

In terms of campus area, Berry College, an American private, liberal arts college in Mount Berry, Georgia, is one of the largest in the world. It hosts approximately 2,100 students on 28,000 acres of beautiful north Georgia hill country. Our expansive campus provides unparalleled opportunities for learning, research, and recreation, fostering a unique educational environment where students can thrive both academically and personally.
